Ownership and Mission

Honest News is an experimental transparency-first news analysis platform focused on showing multiple perspectives side by side. Our mission is to reduce polarization by making framing and emotional tone visible to readers.

How content is produced

We aggregate reporting, run structured analysis, and publish machine-readable outputs with optional file-integrity verification workflows. We disclose methods and limitations publicly.

Contact and accountability

For factual issues, corrections requests, or feedback, use the site owner contact channel maintained for this project. Requests are reviewed and tracked against our corrections policy.
