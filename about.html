About

Independent. Transparent. Verifiable.

The News Meta is built for readers who want more than a headline and less than a partisan script. We show the source mix, the framing differences, and the verification trail behind every story.

What The News Meta does

The News Meta processes each story through left, center, and right coverage, scores the source mix, synthesizes a balanced article, and exposes the reasoning trail in plain language. The goal is not to flatten disagreement. It is to make the shape of coverage visible so readers can inspect it themselves.

Editorial position

We are independent, non-partisan, and methodology-led. We do not ask readers to trust a black box. We ask them to inspect the work: which sources were used, how perspectives were weighted, and where disagreement remains unresolved.

Accountability

The platform publishes its methodology, corrections process, and verification workflow in the open. When a factual or process error is confirmed, we update the relevant page and record the change clearly.
