The Honest News Process

1. Aggregate Diverse Sources

Articles are aggregated from outlets across the political and cultural spectrum to ensure balance and break echo chambers.

2. Structured Text Analysis

Each headline is analyzed with a repeatable scoring process for language tone, framing, and emotional intensity across viewpoints.

3. Extract Framing & Emotion Signals

We score for loaded language, polarization, and framing bias so differences are visible and reviewable.

4. Publish Comparable Metrics

Each story includes simple, comparable metrics so readers can evaluate tone, bias, and confidence at a glance.

5. Optional Integrity Signing

When available, an Ed25519 signature can be used to verify a file has not been modified after publication.

6. Client-Side Verification

Anyone can verify results in their browser using our public key. No hidden models, no secret weights, no opaque decisions.
