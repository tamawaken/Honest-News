Core standards

  • Clear labeling of analysis outputs and non-opinion intent.
  • Source diversity where available and clear disclosure when limited.
  • Method transparency, including known limitations and uncertainty.
  • No silent edits to core analysis outputs after publication.
  • Public correction workflow for factual and technical errors.

Labeling policy

Published outputs are labeled as machine-assisted multi-perspective analysis. They are not legal, medical, or financial advice.

Sourcing policy

We aim to include contrasting perspectives and identify missing coverage. Where possible, article metadata names source outlets and publish dates.

Update policy

Substantive changes should include an update note or correction entry. Transparency takes priority over appearance.
