How It Works

Honest News uses the BoonMind Democratic AI Engine — a 1000-trait voting system that evaluates bias, emotion, and framing across multiple sources.

1. Collect Diverse Sources

Articles are aggregated from outlets across the political and cultural spectrum to ensure balance and break echo chambers.

2. Democratic AI Analysis

Each headline is evaluated by a virtual parliament of 1000 AI traits. The system measures language tone, emotional bias, and framing across viewpoints.

3. Counter-Narrative Detection

The engine automatically identifies alternative perspectives and hidden framing patterns to expose emotional manipulation.

4. Cryptographic Verification

Each analysis is digitally signed to prove authenticity — no hidden weighting or silent edits.

5. Transparency & Limits

This is a research prototype. Outputs reflect the source data’s bias. Use it as a lens for understanding, not a single single truth.
